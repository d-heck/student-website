Web Design - Storefront Solo Project, Jun 2017 - Ongoing To gather a deeper understanding of websites, I merged my love for audio design and web building. I wanted to create a secure, functional, and low cost platform where individuals could download and purchase my custom drum and synthesis kits. The website is hosted on a cPanel server, and is a modified version of wordpress (of which I tailored to my needs). The website is constantly evolving and has already filled out several dozen customer orders.  All of said orders have been automatically handled through the system. The website has all the functionality of a high profile ecommerce storefront, created with only a fraction of the cost. Disclaimer: Although I have put many hours into this project, I have used plug-ins, themes, and APIs. LINK Horror Game Mechanics Solo Project, May 2017 I challenged myself to build all the mechanics needed for a complete horror game. All sound design and production by David Heck, created in the Unreal Engine. Important mechanics to note: - Dynamic flashlight; Responds to environment as a light source. Randomly flickers and dims throughout gameplay. - Leaning; The ability to peer around corners to asses a room. - Sprint & Sneak; The player should be able to crouch low and sneak, while also being able to flee from danger. + View Bobbing; Giving a player the feel of fleeing, helps add tension.
